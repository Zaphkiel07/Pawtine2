# Pawtine Roadmap

## Phase 0 – Foundations (Weeks 0‑2)
- Finalize product vision, KPIs, and success metrics.
- Complete target-user interviews and competitive scan.
- Lock tech stack choices and architecture principles.
- Deliver wireframes, visual language, analytics instrumentation plan, and groomed backlog.
- Launch marketing positioning, waitlist, and survey campaign aligned with `marketing-plan.md`.

## Phase 1 – MVP Build (Weeks 3‑8)
- Implement onboarding flow (dog profile setup, routine suggestions).
- Build automated reminders, habit tracker, weekly dashboard, routine customization, and notification engine.
- Configure analytics, error monitoring, and local/push notifications.
- Run QA smoke tests on priority devices; create regression test checklist.
- Marketing prepares launch assets, nurture drip content, and ad creative concepts.

## Phase 2 – Beta & Polish (Weeks 9‑10)
- Conduct closed beta with representative dog owners; collect qualitative and quantitative feedback on reminders and completion flows.
- Address high-priority usability issues, tune messaging, and polish streak/badge visuals.
- Execute soft marketing push with influencers and waitlist; gather testimonials.
- Finalize app-store metadata, screenshots, and localization copy.

## Phase 3 – Launch Readiness (Weeks 11‑12)
- Complete full regression, performance, and notification reliability testing.
- Finalize Free vs Premium gating, pricing copy, and support documentation.
- Stand up production monitoring dashboards and incident playbooks.
- Marketing runs countdown, press outreach, paid ad flight, and ASO iteration.

## Phase 4 – Post-Launch Growth (Weeks 13‑24)
- Track core KPIs daily/weekly; review cohort retention and reminder response rates.
- Ship retention boosters (streak badges refinement, snooze/reschedule improvements, content nudges).
- Launch referral program, community challenges, and milestone-sharing assets.
- Experiment with Premium upsells (multi-dog profiles, extended stats) and monitor conversion.

## Phase 5 – Expansion & AI Prep (Months 6‑9)
- Research AI routine optimization and health insight capabilities; assess data readiness.
- Prototype personalization engine using accumulated usage history.
- Explore partnerships with vets, trainers, and smart-device vendors.
- Plan internationalization, scalable infrastructure needs, and broader marketing segmentation.

## Milestone Dependencies
- Analytics and notification stack configuration is required before MVP QA.
- Beta feedback review gates app-store submission.
- Premium/paywall hooks need legal and finance approvals before launch experiments.
- AI feature discovery requires at least three months of routine data.

## Team Cadence
- Weekly product/engineering/design stand-up.
- Bi-weekly roadmap review with stakeholders.
- Monthly KPI + marketing growth review to adjust backlog priorities.
